About Spend Wisely

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families to take control of their financial future through smart, simple, and accessible money management tools.

What We Offer

  • Real-time expense tracking
  • Customizable budget planning
  • Multi-currency support (RWF, USD, EUR)
  • Mobile money integration (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money)
  • Detailed financial reports & charts
  • 85% budget alert system
  • PDF report export

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Spend Wisely was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves to understand their money. What started as a personal project has grown into a comprehensive financial platform serving users across Africa and beyond.
